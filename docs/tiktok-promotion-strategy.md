# TikTok Promotion Strategy for InkLink Tattoo Finder

> **Goal**: Drive traffic to [inklinktattoofinder.com](https://inklinktattoofinder.com), grow brand awareness, and attract both tattoo seekers and shop owners to the platform.

---

## Why TikTok?

- Tattoo content is **massively popular** on TikTok — #tattoo has 100B+ views
- The platform skews 18–34, which is the core demographic for tattoo seekers
- Short-form video is the best format for showcasing visual content like tattoo art
- Organic reach on TikTok still far exceeds Instagram and YouTube Shorts

## Production Guide (Low-Budget, High-Impact)

### Tools You Need
| Tool | Purpose | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Smartphone (iPhone/Android) | Recording | Free (you have one) |
| CapCut (app) | Editing, captions, transitions | Free |
| Canva | Thumbnails, text overlays | Free tier works |
| InkLink website | Screen recordings for demos | Free |
| Ring light or window light | Better lighting | $15–30 |
| Phone tripod | Stable shots | $10–20 |

### Screen Recording Tips (for product demos)
1. Use your phone's built-in screen recorder
2. Open InkLink on mobile (it's responsive — this looks great)
3. Scroll smoothly, tap slowly — viewers need to follow along
4. Add voiceover or text overlay in CapCut afterward
5. Keep it under 30 seconds for demos

### Talking Head Tips
1. Look at the camera lens, not the screen
2. Start with the hook in the first 1–2 seconds (no intros!)
3. Use jump cuts to keep energy high
4. Add captions — 80% of TikTok is watched on mute
5. End with a clear CTA ("Link in bio", "Follow for more")

## Growth Tactics

### Quick Wins
- **Comment on popular tattoo videos** with helpful tips + subtle InkLink mentions
- **Duet/stitch tattoo fail videos** — "this is why you research your artist first"
- **Reply to comments with video** — TikTok boosts these
- **Pin your best-performing video** to your profile

### Collaborations
- **Tattoo artists on TikTok**: Send them a free shoutout video → they share it → mutual growth
- **Tattoo influencers**: Offer to feature their recommended shops
- **Local creators**: Partner with city-specific lifestyle creators for "best of [city]" content

### Paid Promotion (When Ready)
- **Spark Ads**: Boost your best organic videos (start with $20–50/day)
- **Target**: 18–35, interests in tattoos, body art, specific cities
- **Best content to boost**: City spotlights and "Find Your Artist" demos (high intent)
